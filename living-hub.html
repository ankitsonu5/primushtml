The Living Well Hub at Primus brings together thoughtful design, smart technology, and global best practices to create environments that support wellness—for every generation.

Whether you're a grandparent needing slip-free flooring, a parent balancing caregiving and career, or a child exploring the world in safe spaces, Primus homes adapt to your life.

Marzi is an all-in-one lifestyle platform designed for India's 55+ community. It lets you travel, stay healthy, learn new things and live independently, all on your own terms. From curated trips and fitness programs to digital concierge services and safe-home solutions, everything is tailored to your comfort and pace. Marzi also offers full stack support based on active ageing principles.
